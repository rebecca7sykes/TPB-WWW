The Performing Ensemble standards are divided into Choir (Vocal), Band (Wind and percussion) and String/Orchestra groupings for
Strand 1: Create based on the specific skills and techniques needed in relation to the instrumentation in the ensemble.
Guitar, keyboard classes, and any other non-traditional ensembles will utilize relevant standards taken from the General Music and
Performing Ensembles standards.
Students may be classified as Beginning, Intermediate, or Advanced within the same grade level, class, and/or performing ensemble.
