ESSENTIALS (Grades 4-8)
Students know and are able to do all of the above and the following:
?
1FL-E1. Comprehend the main idea in authentic oral and written materials on a
familiar topic
?
1FL-E2. Comprehend well-developed paragraphs containing complex sentences and
idiomatic expressions
?
1FL-E3. Comprehend, interpret and analyze the style of a short piece of fiction or
essay on familiar topics
?
1FL-E4. Identify characteristics of a variety of literary genres, e.g., short stories, plays,
essays
?
1FL-E5. Identify emotions and feelings from selected reading material
?
1FL-E6. Read a poem and analyze its components
PROFICIENCY (Grades 9-12)
Students know and are able to do all of the above and the following:
?
1FL-P1. Comprehend the main ideas and significant details in both oral presentations
and written text
?
1FL-P2. Comprehend authentic newspapers and magazine articles
?
1FL-P3. Identify characteristics of a variety of literary genres including poetry
?
1FL-P4. Identify and comprehend cultural nuances, including humor, in written and
spoken language
?
1FL-P5. Analyze and determine the significance of the principal themes and
characteristics of a major literary text
?
1FL-P6. Analyze the styles of two or more authors within one genre
