Thomas M. Haladyna, Ph.D.,

Susan Pimentel, J.D., (Cornell University)
Co-Founder, StandardsWork? Hanover, New Hampshire, specializes in standards-driven school
reform and works as an education writer, analyst, and consultant. After earning a Bachelor of Science degree in early childhood education and a

planning in such varied jurisdictions as Beaufort, SC; Chicago, IL; Red Clay, DE; Jackson, TN, Ardmore, OK, Elaine and Marvell, AR; and the
states of Arizona, California, Georgia, Maryland and Pennsylvania. Ms. Pimentel is co-author with Denis P. Doyle of the best-selling book and
