the manner in which an author presents a character by using the character's actions, dialogue, description, or how other

a five line stanza with successive lines of two, four, six, eight, and two syllables
circular reasoning
supporting a position by merely restating it

and differ from each other or from some standard for evaluation

an elaborate and extended metaphor, especially in poetic images
