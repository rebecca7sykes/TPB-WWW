Strand 1: Reading Process (Grades 11-12)
Reading Process consists of the five critical components of reading, which are Phonemic Awareness, Phonics, Fluency, Vocabulary and
Comprehension of connected text. These elements support each other and are woven together to build a solid foundation of linguistic
understanding for the reader.
