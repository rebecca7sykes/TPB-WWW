Writing Standard
Articulated by Grade Level
GLOSSARY
Venn diagram
a graphic organizer used to help a writer organize ideas to compare and contrast, composed of two
or more overlapping circles [Grade 6]
vowel digraph
a spelling pattern using two or more letters to represent a single vowel sound [Grade 4]
web (webbing)
a type of graphic organizer used to record ideas [Grade 1]
word family (-ies)
words that may have the same root or base sound [Grade 1]
word wall
classroom display of high frequency and/or grade level specific words available for student reference
[Kindergarten]
The grade level shown in brackets at the end of a definition indicates the grade level at which the word first appears in the Standard.
