Social Studies Standard Articulated by Grade Level
Sixth Grade History Strands emphasize World history from its earliest cultures through the Enlightenment, including the
early cultures of the Americas.
Seventh Grade History Strands emphasize American history from the Civil War through the Great Depression. They
study the impact of the Industrial Revolution and imperialism on world events.
Eighth Grade History Strands emphasize the historical foundations and democratic principles that framed our Constitution
and led to our form of democracy. The history of World War II to the contemporary world is also studied.
