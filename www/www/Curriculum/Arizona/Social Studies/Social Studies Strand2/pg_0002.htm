Social Studies Standard Articulated by Grade Level
Strand 2: World History
Concept 1: Research Skills for History
Historical research is a process in which students examine topics or questions related to historical studies and/or current issues. By
using primary and secondary sources effectively students obtain accurate and relevant information. An understanding of chronological
order is applied to the analysis of the interrelatedness of events. These performance objectives also appear in Strand 1: U.S. History.
They are intended to be taught in conjunction with appropriate U.S. or World History content, when applicable.
