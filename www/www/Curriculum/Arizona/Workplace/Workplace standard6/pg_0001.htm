STANDARD 6
Students illustrate how social, organizational and technological systems function.
Definition: A system equals an organized framework made up of interrelated components
acting together as a whole, in which a change in one component may affect the
entire operation. Examples of systems are social (e.g., family, school) and
technological (e.g., local area network, telephone).
