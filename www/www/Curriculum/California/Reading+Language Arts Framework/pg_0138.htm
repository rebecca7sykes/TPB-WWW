Instructional Materials
Instructional materials should carefully sequence the introduction of the di-
mensions of revising and editing. Focus first on the number of objectives intro-
duced, then on the number and range of examples. Are the examples adequate?
Or will you need to invest time creating your own examples? Are assessment
passages and examples included? Assessment tasks should be available for each
phase of assessment: entry-level assessment for instructional planning, monitoring
of progress toward the instructional objective, and post-test assessment toward the
standard.
