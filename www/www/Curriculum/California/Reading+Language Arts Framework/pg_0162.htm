Using the speaking strategies of grade five outlined in Listening and Speaking
Standard 1.0, students:
2.1 Deliver narrative presentations:
a. Establish a situation, plot, point of view, and setting with
descriptive words and phrases.
b. Show, rather than tell, the listener what happens.
2.2 Deliver informative presentations about an important idea, issue, or
event by the following means:
a. Frame questions to direct the investigation.
b. Establish a controlling idea or topic.
c. Develop the topic with simple facts, details, examples, and
explanations.
2.3 Deliver oral responses to literature:
a. Summarize significant events and details.
b. Articulate an understanding of several ideas or images communi-
cated by the literary work.
c. Use examples or textual evidence from the work to support
conclusions.
