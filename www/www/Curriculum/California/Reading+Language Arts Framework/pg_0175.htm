Instructional Materials
Instructional materials should include a liberal quantity of material that teach-
ers can use to teach concepts and procedures for research report writing. For
instance, instructional materials should include detailed examples of develop-
ing index cards or another systematic approach to creating bibliographies. They
should also include detailed guides to resources particularly useful for this stan-
dard (such as Web sites or references written below grade level for English learners
and students who have reading difficulties).
