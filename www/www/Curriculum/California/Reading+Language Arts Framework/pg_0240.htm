he standards for the eleventh and twelfth grades are the pinnacle of all
the standards for the language arts. Most of the standards at this level are
sophisticated extensions of the knowledge and skills previously targeted
in the earlier grades. They highlight several interrelationships among the differ-
ent domains of language arts: reading, writing, written and oral English language
conventions, and speaking and listening. The strands to be emphasized at the
eleventh-grade and twelfth-grade levels are listed above under the appropriate
domains.
