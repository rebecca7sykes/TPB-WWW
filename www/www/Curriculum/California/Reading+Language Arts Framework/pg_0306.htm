28. ELD instructional materials must include the following features:
a. Lessons address the Beginning, Early Intermediate, Intermediate, and
Early Advanced levels of English-language proficiency at appropriate
grade levels.
b. Teacher edition and student materials are focused and efficient to
ensure English learners acquire proficiency in English as quickly and
effectively as possible.
c. Additional ELD instructional support is given to English learners
(beyond the basic program) for one hour daily.
d. The one hour of daily instruction may be presented in smaller seg-
ments or lessons. For example, programs may provide daily instruction
that includes two to four lessons that total one hour per day.
