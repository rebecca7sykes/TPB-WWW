Table 2. Curriculum Content, Grades Four Through Eight
To guide teachers in teaching the technical skills of reading, effective instructional
programs for grades four through eight include the elements listed below. These
elements are presented in an explicit, sequential, linguistically logical, and system-
atic manner.
