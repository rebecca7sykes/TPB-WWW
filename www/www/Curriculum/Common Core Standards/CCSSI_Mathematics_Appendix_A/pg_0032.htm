Unit 3: extending to three Dimensions
Students' experience with two-dimensional and three-dimensional objects is extended to include informal explana-
tions of circumference, area and volume formulas. Additionally, students apply their knowledge of two-dimensional
shapes to consider the shapes of cross-sections and the result of rotating a two-dimensional object about a line.
