positive and negative rational numbers in any form (whole numbers,

operations to calculate with numbers in any form; convert between

mental computation and estimation strategies. For example: If a woman
making $25 an hour gets a 10% raise, she will make an additional 1/10

to place a towel bar 9 3/4 inches long in the center of a door that is 27

edge; this estimate can be used as a check on the exact computation.
