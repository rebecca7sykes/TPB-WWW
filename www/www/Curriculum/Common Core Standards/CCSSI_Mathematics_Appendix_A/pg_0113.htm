Unit 4: expressions and equations
In this unit, students build on their knowledge from unit 2, where they extended the laws of exponents to rational
exponents. Students apply this new understanding of number and strengthen their ability to see structure in and cre-
ate quadratic and exponential expressions. They create and solve equations, inequalities, and systems of equations
involving quadratic expressions.
