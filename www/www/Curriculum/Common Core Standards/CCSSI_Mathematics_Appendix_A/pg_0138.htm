Unit 1: relationships Between Quantities
Work with quantities and rates, including simple linear expressions and equations forms the foundation for this unit.
Students use units to represent problems algebraically and graphically, and to guide the solution of problems. Stu-
dent experience with quantity provides a foundation for the study of expressions, equations, and functions.
