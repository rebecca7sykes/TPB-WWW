How to Read This Document
The materials that follow are divided into text complexity grade bands as defined by the Standards: K每1, 2每3, 4每5, 6每8,
9每10, and 11每CCR. Each band's exemplars are divided into text types matching those required in the Standards for

band and after each text type, sample performance tasks are included for select texts.
