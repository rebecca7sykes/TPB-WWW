Assessment Criteria:
Students could be assessed on their ability to
? improvise and compose simple harmonic accompaniments;
? improvise melodic embellishments and simple rhythmic and melodic
variations on given pentatonic melodies and melodies in major keys;
? improvise short melodies, unaccompanied and over given rhythmic
accompaniments, each in a consistent style, meter, and tonality; and
? compose and arrange short pieces for voices or instruments within
teacher-specified guidelines, using the elements of music to achieve: unity
and variety; tension and release; and balance.
