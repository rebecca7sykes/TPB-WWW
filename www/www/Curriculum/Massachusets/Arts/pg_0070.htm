The Arts Disciplines: Theatre
PreK¨C12 STANDARD 5: Critical Response
Students will describe and analyze their own theatrical work and the work of others using appropriate
theatre vocabulary. When appropriate, students will connect their analysis to interpretation and evaluation.

5.7 Articulate and justify possible criteria for critiquing classroom

5.9 Use appropriate theatre terminology to describe and analyze the strengths
and weaknesses of their own or the group's work
5.10 Give, accept, and use constructive criticism that identifies the specific steps
