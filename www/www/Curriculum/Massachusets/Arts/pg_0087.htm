The Arts Disciplines: Visual Arts
PreK¨C12 STANDARD 4: Drafting, Revising, and Exhibiting
Students will demonstrate knowledge of the processes of creating and exhibiting their own artwork: drafts,
critique, self-assessment, refinement, and exhibit preparation.
