PreK¨C12 STANDARD 8: Concepts of Style, Stylistic Influence, and Stylistic
Change
Students will demonstrate their understanding of the concepts of style, stylistic influence, and stylistic
change by identifying when and where art works were created and by analyzing characteristic features of
art works from various historical periods, cultures, and genres.
