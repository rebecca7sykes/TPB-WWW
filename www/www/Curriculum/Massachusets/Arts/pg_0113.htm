Connections: Sample PreK¨C4 Learning Scenario
Repetition in Music and Dance
Standards:
Connections, Learning Standard 10.1
Music, Learning Standards 2.1, 4.2
Dance, Learning Standard 2.2
Sample
Assessment Criteria:
Students could be assessed on their ability to
? integrate knowledge of music and dance;
? demonstrate and respond to meter and rhythm;
? compose simple rhythmic ostinato accompaniments; and
? create a dance phrase with a beginning, middle, and end and repeat it with
or without music.
Summary:
Exploring the concept of repetition, students listen to an excerpt from Paul
Dukas'
The Sorcerer's Apprentice
and identify a musical theme that is
repeated. They discuss the rhythm and clap an ostinato accompaniment.
Working in pairs, students improvise a dance phrase that echoes the patterns
in the music and perform the work for the class.
