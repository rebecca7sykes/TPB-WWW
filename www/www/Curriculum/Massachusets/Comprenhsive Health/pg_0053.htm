PreK¨C12 STANDARD 9: Safety and Injury Prevention
Students will gain the knowledge and skills to administer first aid and carry out emergency proced ures, including
cardiopulmon ary resuscitation, will avoid, recognize, and report verbal, physical, and emotional abuse situations,
and will assess the factors that contribute to intentional and unintentional injury, including motor vehicle accidents,
fire safety, and weapons safety.
