GENERAL STANDARD 16: Myth, Traditional Narrative, and Classical Literature (Continued)
Students will identify, analyze, and apply knowledge of the themes,
structure, and elements of myths, traditional narratives, and classical literature
and provide evidence from the text to support their understanding.
