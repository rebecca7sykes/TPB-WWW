Listening and Speaking (S)
S.1 Vocabulary: Students will comprehend and communicate orally, using
English vocabulary for personal, social, and academic purposes.
"Our ability to think clearly and communicate with precision depends
on our individual store of words. A rich vocabulary enables students
to understand what they read, and to speak and write with flexibility
and control."
(
Massachusetts English Language Arts Curriculum Framework,
p. 19)
Communicating with single words and word phrases, beginning English
Language Learners of all ages increase their English word knowledge in
strategic and systematic ways. As they advance in proficiency, students
continue to develop their vocabulary in a variety of social and academic
contexts. Substantial word and phrase knowledge provides a successful
base for reading and writing in English.
June 2003 Massachusetts Department of Education
