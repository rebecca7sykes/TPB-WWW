Massachusetts Association of Teachers to Speakers of Other Languages (MATSOL)
www.matsol.org
MATSOL is a non-profit professional organization and the state affiliate of TESOL, the
national association of Teachers of English to Speakers of Other Languages. MATSOL
provides technical assistance and professional development in conjunction with district
improvement plans, district plans for English language learners, and Individual
Professional Development Plans (IPDP) of ESL, Bilingual Education, and content/
classroom teachers working with English language learners.
MATSOL offers an annual fall conference and longer-term professional development
formats. MATSOL publishes a newsletter, journal, and other publications linking research,
policy, and practice.

www.matsol.org
A service offered by the Massachusetts Association of Teachers to Speakers of Other

the field via the MATSOLWORKS listserv.
