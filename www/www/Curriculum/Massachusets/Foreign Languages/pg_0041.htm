Communication Strand:
Sample Stage 1 Learning Scenario
Personal Collage
Standards:
Presentational Communication
Learning Standards 3.1, 3.3
Grade Level:
PreK¨C4, Foreign Language in Elementary School (FLES)
Sample
Assessment Criteria:
Students could be assessed on their ability to communicate in
the target language at Stage 1 proficiency level to:
? express opinions and ideas; and
? express agreement and disagreement.
Summary:
Students create collages representing things they like and
dislike (foods, colors, pets, activities, etc.) by drawing or selecting
pictures from magazines. All images are also labeled in the
target language. Students then pair up for presentations. Each
student makes a series of statements expressing opinions about
the images he or she has chosen to represent. The partner
expresses agreement or disagreement with each statement and
then the roles are reversed. This can also be done as a written
activity; images are posted around the room and students circu-
late to find representations for which they write sentences
expressing agreement or disagreement.
Materials needed:
Dictionaries, sources for vocabulary for each topic; art supplies,
magazines
