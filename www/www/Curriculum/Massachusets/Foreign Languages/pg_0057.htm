Summary:
In conjunction with their history class, small groups of
students research trends in immigration to the United States
since 1800 and trends in immigration to a Latin American coun-
try (such as Argentina) during the same time period to find simi-
larities and differences. They research immigrants' countries of
origin, reasons for immigration, conditions in those countries, the
immigrants' adjustment to the new culture, and the traditions
and products that they brought into their adopted country.
Students present their findings to the whole class
for discussion.
Materials needed:
Print and non-print materials on immigration trends to the
United States and to Latin American countries. Encyclopedia
entries and biographies of famous immigrants or descendants of
immigrants.
