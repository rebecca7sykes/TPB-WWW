Summary:
Using what they have learned about nutrition, food groups, and
eating a balanced diet, students draw comparisons between the
typical American diet and that of the Japanese. They study food
packaging and nutritional charts from Japan and compare them
to our own. They find out how different meals in Japan satisfy
nutritional requirements.
