PRE K每12 STANDARD 8: Communities
Students will use languages other than English within and beyond the school setting. Students of classical languages
will recognize elements of classical languages and ancient cultures in the world around them, and they will share
insights derived from their study of classical languages with others within and beyond the classroom setting.
