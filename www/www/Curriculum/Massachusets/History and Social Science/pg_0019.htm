At the preschool and kindergarten level, learning in history and social science is built on children's
experiences in their families, school, community, state, and country. The picture books chosen for
reading aloud, the stories told, and the songs they hear or learn are basic components of the cur-
riculum. Children listen to stories about the people and events we celebrate in our national holi-
days and learn why we celebrate them. They also become familiar with our national symbols to
help them develop a civic identity.
