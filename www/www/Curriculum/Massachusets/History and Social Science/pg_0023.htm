Second graders study world and United States history, geography, economics, and government by
learning more about who Americans are and where they came from. They explore their own family's
history and listen to or read a variety of teacher- or student-selected stories about: distinctive
individuals, peoples, achievements, customs, events, places, or landmarks from long ago and
around the world. Students learn more economic concepts by identifying producers, consumers,
buyers, and sellers in their own communities.
