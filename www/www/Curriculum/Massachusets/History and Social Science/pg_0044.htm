On a map of the world, locate Southeast Asia, the Indian Ocean, Australia, New
Zealand, Antarctica, the major Pacific Islands, the Pacific Ocean, and the Coral Sea.

Sea, the Great Victoria Desert, and the Great Barrier Reef. (G)

Southeast Asia, Australia, and the major Pacific Islands. (G, E)
