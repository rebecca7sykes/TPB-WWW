Seventh graders study the origins of human beings in Africa and the early civilizations that flourished
in the Mediterranean area. They study the religions, governments, trade, philosophies, and art of these
civilizations as well as the powerful ideas that arose in the ancient world and profoundly shaped the course
of world history. These ideas include monotheism, democracy, the rule of law, individual worth, personal
responsibility, the alphabetic principle for a writing system, and scientific reasoning.
Note: The grade 7 MCAS will cover the world geography, history, economics, and civics standards,
concepts, and skills of grades 6 and 7.
