The 2000 Mathematics Curriculum Framework is one of seven sets of standards created to
advance educational reform in Massachusetts. It is the work of teachers and administra-
tors in pre-kindergarten through grade 12, mathematics education professors, university
mathematicians, and community members working with staff from the Department of
Education.
