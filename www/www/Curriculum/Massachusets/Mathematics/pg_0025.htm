Refers to standards K.N.1, K.N.2, K.N.4, and K.N.8
Have each child estimate the number of seeds in a slice of watermelon by inspection.
Remove and count the seeds and compare the estimate to the count. Children then draw
and color pictures of slices of watermelon, paste the seeds on their drawings, record the
number of seeds, and compare their watermelon slices to tell who has more seeds.
