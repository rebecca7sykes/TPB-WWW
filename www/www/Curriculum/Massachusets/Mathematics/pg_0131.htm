Variable
A letter used to represent one or more numbers in an expression, equation,
inequality, or matrix. (H)
Vector
A quantity that has magnitude and direction. A vector is typically represented
by a directed line segment, whose length represents the magnitude and whose orientation
in space represents the direction. (W)
Venn diagram
A diagram that is used to show relationships between sets. (H)
Vertex-edge graph
A graph consisting of points with connections in which the points
are called vertices and the line segments are called edges.
Whole number
A number that is either a counting number or zero.
