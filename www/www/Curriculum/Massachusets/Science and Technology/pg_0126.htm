Life Science (Biology)
Grades PreK每2, pages 44每45
Standard #1
?
Using string, mark out a circle of about two meters in diameter in the schoolyard or a
nearby park. Have students survey the biodiversity of the circle. Younger students
can look for leaves of different shapes and older students can find out how many
different types of plants and animals are found in the circle. Ask how the living
things in the circle might be different in different seasons, then test predictions by
going out to see.
?
Design and build several cardboard boxes, each of which has a small round opening
at a different location on the box. Cover newly germinating seeds with the various
boxes and observe how the stems grow toward the light that comes through the
openings. (T/E 2.3)
