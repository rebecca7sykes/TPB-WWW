Standard 1: Creating, Performing, and Participating
Performance Indicator 1a
Compose simple pieces that reflect a knowledge of melodic, rhythmic, harmonic, timbrel, and
dynamic elements.
Students compose a theme with three variations in three class
periods.
(Band, Chorus, Orchestra, General Music)
Prior experience: Students will have listened to and discussed theme
and variation.
? Teachers lead brainstorming session in which class lists familiar
poems.
? Class selects one poem to be the basis for the theme and variation
composition.
? Teacher selects a scale to be used to create a theme that
accompanies the natural rhythm of the poem.
? Students explore melodic possibilities of each phrase in sequence.
Under the direction of the teacher, students:
每
Create a melodic theme to go with the natural rhythm of the text.
每
Create accompanying patterns.
每
Perform and notate composed theme and accompanying
patterns.
每
Brainstorm concept of variation elements to vary (meter, tonality,
instruments, style).
每
Choose which element is to be used for variation.
每
Explore ways to vary the theme, focusing on the chosen element.
每
Share ideas and together create variation.
每
Repeat process for variations two and three.
每
Rehearse and perform entire composition.
Suggested Assessment:
?
Class listens to an unfamiliar theme and variation and identifies the
element being varied.
?
Teacher plays a melody that has been composed by the class, and
varies one element. Students identify the element and describe how it
has changed.
Note: Also addresses Standards 2a and 3b.
