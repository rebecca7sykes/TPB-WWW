Standard 2: Knowing and Using Arts Materials and
Resources
Performance Indicator 2e
Demonstrate appropriate listening and other participatory responses to music of a variety of
genres and cultures.
Students develop a chart of appropriate audience behaviors.
(Band, Chorus, Orchestra, General Music)
? Teacher initiates discussion about behaviors when listening to music,
focusing on:
每 Body position (sitting, kneeling, standing, lying down).
每 Movement (being still, conducting, dancing, nodding head).
每 Vocalization (being silent, humming, singing, whispering, talking,
tapping).
每 Food-related actions (eating, drinking, chewing gum, opening
wrappers).
每 Response (applauding, whistling, yelling, standing to applaud).
? Class discusses types of venues, musical events, and cultures, and
talks about how behaviors should change according to the type of
music and culture of musical event.
? Students complete a chart of behaviors for various musical events
and cultures, using the above categories and behaviors as
guidelines.
Suggested Assessment:
?
Attend a school assembly or other musical event and write or present
a review of audience behavior. Assess, using the class-related chart
as a guide.
Note: Also addresses Standard 3d.
Act out a concert scenario, demonstrating incorrect and correct behaviors.
Demonstrate appropriate behavior at school assemblies and concerts, and on music-related field trips.
