Standard 3: Responding to and Analyzing Works of Art
Performance Indicator 3c
Demonstrate a basic awareness of the technical skills musicians must develop to produce an
aesthetically acceptable performance.
Students explore a variety of performing techniques that produce
different timbres.

Students describe in writing how to produce two or more different
sounds on a selected instrument. Teacher adapts writing assessment
