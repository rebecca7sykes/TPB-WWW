such as an auditory enhancement device or a voice-activated computer, with disabilities during music class-

teacher provide successful learning activities for students with disabilities.
The music teacher should expect students with disabilities to successfully reach the goals established for
music class as well as those indicated on their IEPs. The music teacher, as well as the student's other regu-
