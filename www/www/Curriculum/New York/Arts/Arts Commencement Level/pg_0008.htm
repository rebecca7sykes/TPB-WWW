Performance Indicator 2a
Use traditional, electronic, and nontraditional media for composing, arranging, and performing
music.
Students arrange a quartet.
(Band, Chorus, Orchestra)
? Teacher supplies several chorales that are written for
instruments/voices other than what the students perform (e.g., vocal
students could choose a brass chorale).
? Teacher sets up a manuscript template as a quartet with appropriate
number of measures per line to assist the students in organization.
? If the chorale is written for transposing instruments, the teacher
identifies those on the score.
? Students:
每 Choose chorale.
每 Write in key and time signatures.
每 Identify what part will be performed by what instrument or voice.
每 Write parts, being aware of clef, octave, and transposition.
每 Add articulations, dynamics, etc.
每 Enter arrangement on a notation program. Perform, play back,
listen, check, and correct.
? Student chorale may be used as part of daily warm-ups.
? Students conduct their arrangements.
Suggested Assessment:
?
Teacher uses rubric addressing accurate use of key and time
signatures, notation, and articulation and dynamics. Rubrics should
also address performance and conducting technique.
Note: Also addresses Standards 1a, 1b, and 1c.
