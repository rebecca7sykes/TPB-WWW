Contributions of the Arts
Performance Indicator 4c
Identify the primary cultural, geographical, and historical settings for the music they listen to and
perform.
Students identify music that reflects specific geographic,
historical, and cultural traditions.
? Through singing and experiencing songs of diverse cultural
backgrounds, students share geographic, historical, and cultural
traditions.

? Teacher shares the story of the fishing canoes and the Polynesian
fishermen's use of the song "Tongo" to hear the location of the other
canoes (e.g., if the echo is loud, the canoe is close¡ªwatch out!).
? The canoe motions add an authentic feel to the rhythm and beat of
the piece.
? Students can hold rhythm sticks to represent the paddles.
? Other students can wave scarves or large, sheet-size pieces of cloth
to represent the water.

?
Students describe their comprehension of the cultural context of
"Tongo" through accurate movement, correct pronunciation, and
