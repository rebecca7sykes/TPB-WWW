Standard 1: Creating, Performing, and Participating
Performance Indicator 1d
In performing ensembles, read very easy/easy music (NYSSMA Level I-II) and respond appropriately
to the gestures of the conductor.
Using a simple two-part piece of music, make dynamic and tempo
changes not printed in the music and determine whether students
respond.
