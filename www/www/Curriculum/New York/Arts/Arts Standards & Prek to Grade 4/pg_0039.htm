Demonstrate appropriate audience behavior, including attentive listening, in a variety of musical
settings in and out of school.
Students compare and contrast expected audience behavior in var-
ious musical settings: listening to a school concert in an
auditorium; seeing a marching band in a parade; listening to music
in a church or synagogue; etc.
