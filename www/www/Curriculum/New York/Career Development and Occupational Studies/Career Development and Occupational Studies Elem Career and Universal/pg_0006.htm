Performance Indicator 7:
Students describe the changing roles of men and women at home and in the
workplace.
Students will:
A. Identify their roles in the home environment and workplace.
B. List the roles and occupations of men and women in the workplace:
1. Distinguish between traditional and nontraditional roles
and occupations of men and women at home and in the
workforce
2. Discuss the value of work in the home and workplace
C. Identify the factors that affect change in the work environment
(e.g., assembly line vs. shared decision-making process, downsizing,
technological advancement).
Discussion Questions
