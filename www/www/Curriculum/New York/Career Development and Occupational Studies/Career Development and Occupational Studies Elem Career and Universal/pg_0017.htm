ELEMENTARY SAMPLE ACTIVITY
Essential Question(s):
Who am I?
Title of Activity:
Me Bags
K 1 2 3 4 Estimated Time:
per student 5¨C10 minutes
OBJECTIVE(S)
Students will increase awareness of self and others.
DESCRIPTION OF ACTIVITY
? Students take home to parents a letter explaining that the students
are working on a project to help the students better understand
themselves and others by creating a "Me Bag." They also take home a
paper bag and a copy of the following "Make a Me Bag" instructions:
?
What words or things describe you?
?
Place at least three but not more than five things in a paper bag!
Suggested items are photos, magazine pictures, souvenirs, small
toys, things you collect, etc.
?
Bring this bag to school with you on Monday to share with the class!
? During a sharing session in class, each student takes the items out
of the bag and takes a few minutes to explain why he/she chose
those items.
? Students receive a copy of the class list and write one thing
they heard each of their classmates share.
MATERIALS/RESOURCES
? Letter to parents
? Instruction sheet
? Brown paper bags (lunch size)
COMMENTS/MODIFICATIONS
? Activity is recommended for use at beginning of school year.
ASSESSMENT/EVALUATION
? Students communicate information among themselves.
SOURCE/CREDIT
Submitted by Antoinette M. Howard, Forest Park Elementary School, South Colonie Central School District.
