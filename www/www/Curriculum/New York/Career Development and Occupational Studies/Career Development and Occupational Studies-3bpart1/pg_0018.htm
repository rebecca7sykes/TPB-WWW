Mind Your Own Business
Sample Debrief
1. How did you choose your group leader/manager?
2. What leadership traits does this person have to make him/her effective as the person "in charge"?
3. What changes did you notice in the workplace you participated in as the activity progressed?
4. What reactions did you have to being laid off from your position?
5. What reactions did you have to a new person coming into your workplace and/or to losing a team member?
6. How did the changes in personnel influence your group's interaction and/or progress?
7. What reactions did you have to the changes in your original workplace at the end of the activity?
