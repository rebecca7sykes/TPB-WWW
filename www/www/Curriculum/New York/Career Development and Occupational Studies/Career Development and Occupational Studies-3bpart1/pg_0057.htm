Engineering/Technologies
Title of Activity:
Workplace Cleanup
Estimated Time:
2 class periods
OBJECTIVE(S)
Students apply engineering/technologies principles for developing a
clean and safe work environment.
DESCRIPTION OF ACTIVITY
Proper cleanup procedures are essential for a safe work environment.
Students research performance standards and federal and state
regulations that apply to cleanup procedures in an engineering/
technologies cluster. Using this information, they perform the following
activities:
? Brainstorm in class discussion to answer the questions:
?
Why is a clean work area important?
?
What are the features of a clean work area?
? Break into work groups to develop a plan to be submitted for class
approval on applying cleanup principles to the classroom or work
setting.
? Modify the plan on the basis of peer feedback.
? Perform trial cleanup in classroom or work setting.
? Evaluate performance, using industry-accepted performance
standards.
? Prepare a summary report as if serving as a safety inspector.
MATERIALS/RESOURCES
? Cleanup tools and materials
