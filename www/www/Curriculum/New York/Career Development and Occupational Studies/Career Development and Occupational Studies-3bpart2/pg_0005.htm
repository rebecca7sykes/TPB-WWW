Performance Indicator:
Students demonstrate how to interact effectively and sensitively with others.
Students will:
A. Understand characteristics and selected aspects of relationships:
1. Group dynamics
2. Independence/interdependence
3. Interrelationship of life roles
4. Conflict resolution
5. Tolerance toward other individuals and groups
6. Effects of change
7. Crisis management
8. Employee relations
9. Customer relations
10. Human resource specialists
B. Understand leadership as related to:
1. Personal development
2. Initiative and responsibility
3. Cooperative work techniques
4. Contributions of individual to group efforts
5. Organizational structures
6. Youth leadership organizations
C. Identify community resources:
1. Agencies (e.g., United Way, Chamber of Commerce)
2. Support systems/networks
3. Professional organizations
4. Individuals (e.g., mentors, community leaders)
