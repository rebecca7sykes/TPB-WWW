Performance Indicator:
Students apply personal and resource management skills.
Students will:
A. Understand and practice personal management:
1. Aptitudes and skills
2. Career plan
3. Time allocation: work, family, leisure, and personal
4. Role models, mentors, and networks
5. Lifelong learning/continuing education
B. Understand and practice resource management:
1. Time
2. Work spaces
3. Human resources
4. Technology
5. Finances
6. Work attire and equipment
7. Conservation of energy, supplies, and materials
C. Understand and practice balancing work and family roles:
1. Work and family schedule
2. Physical and emotional demands
3. Family and social demands
4. Travel requirements
5. Climate of the workplace (e.g.,"family-friendly")
