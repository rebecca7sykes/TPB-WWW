Title of Activity:
Soil Survey
Estimated Time:
2
class periods
OBJECTIVE(S)
Students determine and compare texture and pH in soil samples.
DESCRIPTION OF ACTIVITY
? Students collect three different samples of soil, preferably from areas
different in land use (e.g., home, school, public parks, business
districts, areas with high pedestrian traffic, etc.) and determine soil
texture and pH for each.
? Students chart results in a table or graph, comparing differences.
? Students evaluate how these characteristics may affect plant growth,
cultural practices, and soil management practices.
? Students offer explanations for differences between the soil samples
and explain how these differences are related to land use.
? Finally, they summarize their results in oral presentations to the class.
MATERIALS/RESOURCES
? Soil samples (collected by students)
? pH test kits
COMMENTS/MODIFICATIONS
? Soil samples (collected by students)
? pH test kit
