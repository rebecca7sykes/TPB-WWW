ELEMENTARY SAMPLE ACTIVITY
Content Area(s):
English Language Arts, Mathematics, The Arts
Title of Activity:
Who Is the Boss?
K 1 2
Estimated Time:
3 ¨C 4 days
OBJECTIVE(S)
Students will develop a business plan.
DESCRIPTION OF ACTIVITY
This activity provides students with an opportunity to think creatively as
they develop and construct an imaginary business. Opportunities to
further develop writing skills come about as students write in their
career journals.
? A representative from a bank visits the class to discuss the purpose
and components of a business plan.
? Students work with the teacher to identify a rubric for a business plan.
? Students choose a business they would like to own. The business
should be a business that a student could run.
? Students write a business plan, answering the following questions:
?
What will you sell?
?
What are the prices?
?
How will you get customers?
?
How will you advertise?
? If students are making a product, have them make a prototype of
the product.
? Students present their business plans to the bank representative or
business owners, who can give them feedback and additional assistance.
? Local business owners speak to students about their businesses.
MATERIALS/RESOURCES
? Computer
? Word processing and desktop publishing software
? Paper for newspapers
? Art supplies for banners and other advertisements
? Graphic organizer handouts
COMMENTS/MODIFICATIONS
? Using a digital camera will facilitate inserting the pictures into the
newspaper.
ASSESSMENT/EVALUATION
? Students are evaluated on final product and classroom participation.
