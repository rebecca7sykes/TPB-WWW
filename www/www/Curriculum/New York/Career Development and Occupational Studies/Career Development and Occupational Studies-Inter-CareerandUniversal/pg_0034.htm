INTERMEDIATE SAMPLE ACTIVITY
Essential Question(s):
How do I develop the skills and abilities that I need to be successful in a career? Why do
the choices I make now matter to my future?
Title of Activity:
Being Heart Smart: A Study of the Cardiovascular System
5 6 7 8
Estimated Time:
long-term project
OBJECTIVE(S)
Students will know and understand the purpose and function of the
cardiovascular system.
Students will know and understand the importance of maintaining a
healthy heart.
Students will develop knowledge of careers and career skills associated
with the maintenance and care of the cardiovascular system.
DESCRIPTION OF ACTIVITY
? As a culminating activity for a unit on the cardiovascular system, students
are presented with the American Medical Association (AMA) activity
sheet (see page 101), which outlines the three-part project that the students
are to complete.
? Students are divided into groups to complete the project.
? A timeline is developed, identifying the class time that will be spent on the
project, the various parts to be completed, and the final due date.
? Teacher and students collaborate on the development of rubrics for the
three parts of the project.
?
Project meets and/or exceeds the stated requirements for the project.
?
Project contains many examples of original thought.
?
Information included is accurate and up-to-date.
?
Project is eye-catching in its design.
?
Teams are on task at all times.
?
Teams make efficient use of resources (e.g., time, materials).
?
Individual effort strongly contributes to the group's success.
? Students research the topics and complete the projects.
? Using appropriate research and technology, student groups should
complete the project within one week.
