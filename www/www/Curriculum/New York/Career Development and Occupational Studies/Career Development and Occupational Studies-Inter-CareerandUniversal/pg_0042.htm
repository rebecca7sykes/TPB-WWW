INTERMEDIATE SAMPLE ACTIVITY
Essential Question(s):
How do I find out what I want to know? How do I affect the systems within which I live
and work?
Title of Activity:
Take Me Out to the Ball Park
5 6 7 8
Estimated Time:
long-term project
OBJECTIVE(S)
Students will demonstrate an understanding of the concept of scale by
completing a detailed scale drawing.
Students will demonstrate an awareness of careers necessary to complete
a large community task.
DESCRIPTION OF ACTIVITY
? Students will discuss why baseball is the "Great American Pastime"
and how it may have changed since the turn of the century.
? Students will discuss the World Series, how teams get there, and the
current format.
? Students will be introduced to "Home Field Advantage" project.
? A timeline will be set for completion of the project.
? Guidelines for the final oral presentation will be discussed.
MATERIALS/RESOURCES
? "Home Field Advantage" (see page 109) activity sheet
? Examples of maps and scale drawings
? Current atlases of states and roads
? Local architects and contractors
? Drawing tools
? Calculators
COMMENTS/MODIFICATIONS
? The economics piece of this lesson could be expanded to include such
things as:
?
Impact on local economy (positive and negative)
?
Cost to build the park
?
Breakdown of the cost of a ticket to see where the money goes, etc.
?
The amount of property tax that such a structure would be charged
?
The jobs that such a place would create/eliminate
?
Current salaries of players and why they are paid that amount
? This project can be "shrunk" to meet the needs of lower grades or expanded
to meet the needs of higher grades.
? When possible, local experts should be brought in to work with students.
This is especially true in locations where architects and professional
commercial builders are available.
? To authenticate this project, final drawings could be submitted to a
commercial construction firm to get an estimated cost.
? As a technology option, advanced students could complete their drawings,
using a CAD system.
ASSESSMENT/EVALUATION
? "Home Field Advantage" project will be graded on the basis of rubric
standards that will include:
?
Accuracy of scale
?
Accuracy of required areas
?
Teamwork
?
Presentation of final products
?
Accuracy, neatness, and appeal of design
?
Knowledge of jobs necessary to complete the ball park project
