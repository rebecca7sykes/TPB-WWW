INTERMEDIATE SAMPLE ACTIVITY
Essential Question(s):
How do I affect the systems within which I live and work?
Title of Activity:
Community Services Resource
5 6 7 8
Estimated Time:
1 week
OBJECTIVE(S)
Students will explore available public services and agencies within their
community.
DESCRIPTION OF ACTIVITY
? Teacher explains to the class that they are going to create a
community service resource to distribute to school counselors and
resource centers.
? Students brainstorm a list of known public services and agencies
within the community.
? Students use telephone books, the Internet, and other resources to
complete the list, identifying unknown services/agencies.
? Students work in teams to research information about each agency/
service. Students collaborate to identify the types of information
they need to obtain.
? Students interview counselors to determine the more appropriate
format for their community service resource, and they establish a
design for the publication. Students work with the teacher to develop
a rubric for the resource.
? Students use desktop publishing software to create the community
service resource.
? Students write an introductory letter explaining the purpose of the
resource, and they distribute the resource.
MATERIALS/RESOURCES
? Computer
? Internet access
? Desktop publishing software
? Phone books, additional sources of community information
COMMENTS/MODIFICATIONS
? Guest speakers can be invited to speak about the importance of
community service agencies.
? Students can visit various community service agencies.
? Students can job-shadow representatives from community service
agencies.
ASSESSMENT/EVALUATION
? Evaluation of resource document is based on established rubric.
? Letters explaining the purpose of the resource are evaluated.
? Classroom participation is evaluated.
