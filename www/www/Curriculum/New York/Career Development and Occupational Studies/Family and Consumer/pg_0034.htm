4. Positive interpersonal qualities lead to teamwork
and cooperation in large and small groups in family,
social, and work situations.
Students:
? communicate effectively and help others to learn a new
skill.
This is evident, for example, when students:
demonstrate how to respond effectively to a dissatisfied customer
assist in the teaching of an acquired skill in an elementary/
middle school class or business environment
provide feedback to others in a group project
participate in a job interview.
