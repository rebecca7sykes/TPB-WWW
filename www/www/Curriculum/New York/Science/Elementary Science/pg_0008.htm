Elementary Science
It should be a goal of the instructor to foster the development of science process skills. The application of these
skills allows students to investigate important issues in the world around them.
Inquiry-based units will include many or most of the following process skills. These process skills should be
incorporated into students' instruction as developmentally appropriate.
Classifying
每 arranging or distributing objects, events, or information representing objects or events in classes
according to some method or system
Communicating
每 giving oral and written explanations or graphic representations of observations
Comparing and contrasting
每 identifying similarities and differences between or among objects, events, data,
systems, etc.
Creating models
每 displaying information, using multisensory representations
Gathering and organizing data
每 collecting information about objects and events which illustrate a specific
situation
Generalizing
每 drawing general conclusions from particulars
Identifying variables
每 recognizing the characteristics of objects or factors in events that are constant or change
under different conditions
Inferring
每 drawing a conclusion based on prior experiences
Interpreting data
每 analyzing data that have been obtained and organized by determining apparent patterns or
relationships in the data
Making decisions
每 identifying alternatives and choosing a course of action from among the alternatives after
basing the judgment for the selection on justifiable reasons
Manipulating materials
每 handling or treating materials and equipment safely, skillfully, and effectively
Measuring
每 making quantitative observations by comparing to a conventional or nonconventional standard
Observing
每 becoming aware of an object or event by using any of the senses (or extensions of the senses) to
identify properties
Predicting
每 making a forecast of future events or conditions expected to exist
Note: As an example, these processes are applied in the three key ideas in Standard 1, which outline scientific inquiry. Inquiry
may proceed in a cyclical pattern, with students moving from Key Idea 1 to Key Idea 3 and back to 1 again.
