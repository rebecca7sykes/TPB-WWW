Science is an ongoing process. Most often there is a question or problem that initiates an investigation searching
for a possible solution or solutions. There is no single prescribed scientific method to govern an investigation. It is
important that students practice the skills outlined below. For younger students, the emphasis is on discovery. For
older students, the emphasis is on formulating and investigating their own questions.
