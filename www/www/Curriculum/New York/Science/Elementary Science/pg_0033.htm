Changes in the properties or materi-

Energy exists in various forms: heat,
electric, sound, chemical, mechani-
cal, light.
Energy can be transferred from one
place to another.
Some materials transfer energy better
than others (heat and electricity).
Energy and matter interact: water is
evaporated by the Sun's heat; a bulb
is lighted by means of electrical cur-
rent; a musical instrument is played
to produce sound; dark colors may
absorb light, light colors may reflect
light.
Electricity travels in a closed circuit.
Heat can be released in many ways,
for example, by burning, rubbing
(friction), or combining one sub-
stance with another.
Interactions with forms of energy
can be either helpful or harmful.

energy being changed to another.
