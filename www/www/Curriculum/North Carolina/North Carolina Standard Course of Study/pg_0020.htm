NORTH CAROLINA
ACADEMIC SCHOLARS PROGRAM
History
In March, 1983, the State Board of Education approved the North Carolina Scholars Program to
begin with the 1983-1984 school year. In March, 1990, the State Board of Education revised the
program and re-designated it the North Carolina Academic Scholars Program. Again, the State
Board of Education revised program requirements in August 2002 to make it more consistent
with graduation requirements and promote rigorous academic study. The current plan will
remain in effect for students who entered ninth grade for the first time in or before 2002-2003.
The revised plan is effective for students who enter the ninth grade for the first time in or after
2003-2004. Students who complete the requirements for an academically challenging high
school program will be named North Carolina Academic Scholars and receive special
recognition.
