If our civilization is to be both dynamic and nurturing, its success will
ultimately depend on how well we develop the capacities of our
children, not only to earn a living in our complex world, but to live a
life rich in meaning. To achieve this quality of life requires a vital
connection to one or more of the arts disciplines, which like any
subject, demand discipline and study.
This document identifies what children must
know
and be able to
do
as a result of a comprehensive arts education. A mere nodding
acquaintance with the arts is not enough to sustain children's interest
or involvement in them. The arts will help them discover who they
are, and what is even more important, who they can become.
