These sections come under the goals and begin with a heading in bold
print at the left of the page and entitled "Objectives" with the
objectives following underneath and numbered "1.01, 1.08, 1.13, etc."
The objectives are stated as phrases which start with a capitalized verb
such as Apply, Describe, Perform, Demonstrate, etc. as if completing
the sentence "The learner will know or be able to ...."
Because the objectives are developed for each grade level and high
school course, some of them are repeated as appropriate at ensuing
grade or course levels to suggest continuation of study or ongoing
advancement of knowledge and/or skills. However, when this occurs,
the intent is that the content, instruction, student outcomes and
methods of evaluation should increase in sophistication at each
succeeding grade or course level.
