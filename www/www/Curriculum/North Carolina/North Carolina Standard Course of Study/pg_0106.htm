Unison
: Movements which are performed simultaneously and identically by more than one
dancer.
Unity
: A feeling of wholeness in a dance which is achieved when all of the parts work well
together.
Variation
: Manipulation of the original movement without losing the intent and character.
Warm-up
: Movements and/or movement phrases designed to raise the core body temperature,
move the body through a preparatory range of movement, and bring the mind into focus for
the dance.
