COMPETENCY GOAL 9: The learner will understand music in relation to history and
culture.
(National Standard 9)
Objectives
9.01 Classify unfamiliar yet representative examples of music using distinguishing
characteristics to identify genre, style, culture and/or historical periods; justify the
reasons for the classifications.
9.02 Explain how music has evolved over time.
9.03 Examine patterns, relationships, and trends in music of various cultures and
historical periods to draw inferences and make predictions about social outcomes.
9.04 Show respect for music from various cultures and time periods.
