COMPETENCY GOAL 8: The learner will develop an awareness of art as an
avocation and profession.
Objectives
8.01 Begin to understand different ways people can make a living from creating works of
art.
8.02 Discuss the involvement in art of people of all ages to include visiting museums,
making art, reading or writing about art and artists, buying art, and teaching and
volunteering in the arts.
