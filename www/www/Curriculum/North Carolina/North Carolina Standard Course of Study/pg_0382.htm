Grades K-2 Emphasis
Students develop and refine oral language skills while learning
basic written language skills and use of media. They develop
understandings, knowledge, strategies, and skills which enable them to
become independent readers and writers who can continue learning
throughout the years to follow.
Grades 3-5 Emphasis
Students continue to develop basic language skills and develop
the use of these skills to learn content in other disciplines such as
science, social studies, and the arts. They use a variety of media
to demonstrate their learning.
