Competency
Goal 5
The learner will interpret and evaluate representative texts to deepen
understanding of literature of the United States.
5.01 Interpret the significance of literary movements as they have evolved
through the literature of the United States by:
?
analyzing the characteristics of literary genres, including fiction, non-
fiction, drama, and poetry, and how the selection of genre shapes
meaning.
?
relating ideas, styles, and themes within literary movements of the
United States.
?
understanding influences that progress through the literary
movements of the United States.
?
evaluating the literary merit and/or historical significance of a work
from Colonial Literature, the Romantic Era, Realism, the Modern
Era, and Contemporary Literature.
