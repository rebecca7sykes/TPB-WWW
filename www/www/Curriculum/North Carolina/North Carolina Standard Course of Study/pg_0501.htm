Competency
Goal 6
The learner will demonstrate understanding and mastery of standard
written English and exhibit stylistic maturity.
6.01
Demonstrate an understanding of the conventions of language by:
?
employing appropriate grammar and mechanics.
?
applying knowledge of diction.
?
revising writing to enhance voice and style, sentence variety,
subtlety of meaning, and tone in consideration of questions being
addressed, purpose, audience, and genres.
6.02
Exhibit stylistic maturity by:
?
using an effective writing process.
?
utilizing a variety of sentence structures.
?
incorporating clear transitions.
?
developing and appropriately using a wide-ranging vocabulary.
?
controlling rhetorical devices effectively, including tone, voice,
and diction.
