CONTENT OF A READING AND LITERATURE PROGRAM K-12*
A balanced reading and literature program requires that students experience a variety of literary
forms and genres. The pages that follow suggest many opportunities for helping students gain inde-
pendence in reading for aesthetic and personal response, for information, and for critical
analysis and evaluation.
