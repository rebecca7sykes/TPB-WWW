COMPETENCY GOAL 1: The learner will recognize, model, and write whole
numbers through 30.
Objectives
1.01 Develop number sense for whole numbers through 30.
a)
Connect model, number word (orally), and number, using a variety of
representations.
b)
Count objects in a set.
c)
Read and write numerals.
d)
Compare and order sets and numbers.
e)
Use ordinals (1st-10th).
f)
Estimate quantities fewer than or equal to 10.
g)
Recognize equivalence in sets and numbers 1-10.
1.02 Share equally (divide) between two people; explain.
1.03 Solve problems and share solutions to problems in small groups.
