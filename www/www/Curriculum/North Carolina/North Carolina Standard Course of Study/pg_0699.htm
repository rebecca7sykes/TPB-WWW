COMPETENCY GOAL 1: The learner will read, write, model, and compute with
whole numbers through 999.
Objectives
1.01 Develop number sense for whole numbers through 999.
a)
Connect model, number word, and number using a variety of
representations.
b)
Read and write numbers.
c)
Compare and order.
d)
Rename.
e)
Estimate.
f)
Use a variety of models to build understanding of place value (ones, tens,
hundreds).
