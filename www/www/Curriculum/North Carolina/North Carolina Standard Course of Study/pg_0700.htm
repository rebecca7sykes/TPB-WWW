Mathematics
1.02 Use area or region models and set models of fractions to explore part-whole
relationships in contexts.
a)
Represent fractions (halves, thirds, fourths) concretely and symbolically.
b)
Compare fractions (halves, thirds, fourths) using models.
c)
Make different representations of the same fraction.
d)
Combine fractions to describe parts of a whole.
1.03 Create, model, and solve problems that involve addition, subtraction, equal
grouping, and division into halves, thirds, and fourths (record in fraction
form).
1.04 Develop fluency with multi-digit addition and subtraction through 999 using
multiple strategies.
a)
Strategies for adding and subtracting numbers.
b)
Estimation of sums and differences in appropriate situations.
c)
Relationships between operations.
1.05 Create and solve problems using strategies such as modeling, composing and
decomposing quantities, using doubles, and making tens and hundreds.
1.06 Define and recognize odd and even numbers.

Objectives
2.01 Estimate and measure using appropriate units.
a)

Objectives
3.01 Combine simple figures to create a given shape.

and rearranged.

a)
Symmetric figures.

Congruent figures.
