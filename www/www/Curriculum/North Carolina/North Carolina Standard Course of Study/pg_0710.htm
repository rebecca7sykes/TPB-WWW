Algebra
COMPETENCY GOAL 1: The learner will understand and compute with non-
negative rational numbers.
Objectives
1.01 Develop number sense for rational numbers 0.001 through 999,999.
a)
Connect model, number word, and number using a variety of
representations.
b)
Build understanding of place value (thousandths through hundred
thousands).
c)
Compare and order rational numbers.
d)
Make estimates of rational numbers in appropriate situations.
1.02 Develop fluency in adding and subtracting non-negative rational numbers
(halves, fourths, eighths; thirds, sixths, twelfths; fifths, tenths, hundredths,
thousandths; mixed numbers).
a)
Develop and analyze strategies for adding and subtracting numbers.
b)
Estimate sums and differences.
c)
Judge the reasonableness of solutions.
