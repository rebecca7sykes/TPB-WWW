Students will solve relevant and authentic
problems using appropriate technology and
apply these concepts as well as those
developed in earlier years
Strands:
Number and Operations, Measurement, Geometry, Data Analysis and Probability,
Algebra
COMPETENCY GOAL 1: The learner will understand and compute with rational
numbers.
Objectives
1.01 Develop and use ratios, proportions, and percents to solve problems.
1.02 Develop fluency in addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division of
rational numbers.
a)
Analyze computational strategies.
b)
Describe the effect of operations on size.
c)
Estimate the results of computations.
d)
Judge the reasonableness of solutions.
1.03 Develop flexibility in solving problems by selecting strategies and using
mental computation, estimation, calculators or computers, and paper and
pencil.
