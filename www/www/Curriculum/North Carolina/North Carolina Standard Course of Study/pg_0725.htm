Mathematics
Algebra
Students will be expected to describe and translate among graphic,
algebraic, numeric, and verbal representations of relations and use those
representations to solve problems. They use symbols to represent
variables, parameters, and functions and extend their use of symbols to
include vectors and matrices. Students use technology to assist in
developing models and analytical solutions. They use appropriate
terminology and notation to define function, domain, range, composition,
and inverses of functions. They expand their understanding of functions
to include power, polynomial, exponential, periodic, piece-wise, and
recursively defined functions. They solve equations, inequalities, and
systems using algebraic, tabular, numerical, and graphical methods.
