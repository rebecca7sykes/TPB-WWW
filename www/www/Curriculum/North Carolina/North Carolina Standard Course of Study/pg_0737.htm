Technical Mathematics 2
Technical Mathematics 2 continues students' study of algebra and geometry, building upon
middle and high school topics. Geometry, functions, and statistical methods for estimation
and prediction are the topics to be studied in an application-centered environment.
Appropriate technology, from manipulatives to calculators and application software, should
be used regularly for instruction and assessment.
