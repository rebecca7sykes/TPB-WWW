Discrete Mathematics
Discrete Mathematics introduces students to the mathematics of networks, social choice, and
decision making. The course extends students' application of matrix arithmetic and
probability. Applications and modeling are central to this course of study. Appropriate
technology, from manipulatives to calculators and application software, should be used
regularly for instruction and assessment.
