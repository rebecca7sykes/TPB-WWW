Integrated Mathematics 1
Integrated Mathematics 1 provides students the opportunity to study traditional topics from
algebra, geometry, probability, and statistics in a problem-centered, connected approach.
Students will be expected to describe and translate among graphic, algebraic, numeric,
tabular, and verbal representations of relationships and use those representations to solve
problems. Appropriate technology, from manipulatives to calculators and application
software, should be used regularly for instruction and assessment.
