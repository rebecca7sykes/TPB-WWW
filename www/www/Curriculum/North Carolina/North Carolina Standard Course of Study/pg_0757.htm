Advanced Placement Calculus
Advanced Placement Calculus develops the student's understanding of the concepts of
calculus (functions, graphs, limits, derivatives and integrals) and provides experience with its
methods and applications. The course encourages the geometric, numerical, analytical, and
verbal expression of concepts, results, and problems. Appropriate technology, from
manipulatives to calculators and application software, should be used regularly for
instruction and assessment.
