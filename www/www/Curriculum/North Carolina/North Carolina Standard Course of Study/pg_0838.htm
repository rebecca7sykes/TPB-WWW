appropriate technologies and information systems to build an understanding of the
atmosphere.
Objectives
3.01 Explain the composition, properties and structure of the atmosphere:
?
Mixture of gases.
?
Stratified layers.
?
Each layer has distinct properties.
?
As altitude increases, air pressure decreases.
?
Equilibrium.

quality:
?
Particulate matter.
?
Ozone.

Air quality standards.
?

?

?
Local air quality issues.

Tornados.
