Physics
PHYSICS - Grades 9-12
Strands:
The strands are: Nature of Science, Science as Inquiry, Science and
Technology, Science in Personal and Social Perspectives. They provide the context for
teaching of the content Goals and Objectives.
COMPETENCY GOAL 1: The learner will develop abilities necessary to do and
understand scientific inquiry.
Objectives
1.01 Identify questions and problems that can be answered through scientific
investigations.
1.02 Design and conduct scientific investigations to answer questions about the
physical world.

Physics, the most fundamental of the natural sciences, is quantitative in nature
and uses the language of mathematics to describe natural phenomena. Inquiry is
applied to the study of matter and energy and their interaction. Learners will
study natural and technological systems. The program strands and unifying
concepts provide a context for teaching content and process skill goals. All
