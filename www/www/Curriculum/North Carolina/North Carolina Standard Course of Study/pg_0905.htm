COMPETENCY GOAL 7: The learner will develop an understanding of wave
motion and the wave nature of sound and light.
7.01 Analyze, investigate, and evaluate the relationship among the characteristics of
waves:
?
Wavelength.
?
Frequency.
?
Period.
?
Amplitude.
7.02 Describe the behavior of waves in various media.
7.03 Analyze the behavior of waves at boundaries between media:
?
Reflection, including the Law of Reflection.
?
Refraction, including Snell's Law.
7.04 Analyze the relationship between the phenomena of interference and the principle
of superposition.
7.05 Analyze the frequency and wavelength of sound produced by a moving source
(the Doppler Effect).
