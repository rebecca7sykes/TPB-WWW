IB Program
In addition to the above programs, some school systems are
implementing an International Baccalaureate (IB) program at the
middle school level. Middle school IB programs are designed to feed
into the high school IB program with its own curriculum. Some
modifications to the Standard Course of Study may be needed to
meet the requirements of the IB program.
