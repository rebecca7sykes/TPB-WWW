COMPETENCY GOAL 3: CONNECTIONS
¨C
The student will relate Latin and the
Greco-Roman world to other disciplines.
Objectives
3.01
Predict the different technical meanings of the specialized vocabulary of other
disciplines, using the knowledge of Greek and Latin roots, prefixes, and
suffixes.
3.02
Analyze implicit and explicit allusions to Greco-Roman mythology and history
in literature, the arts, and other disciplines.
3.03 Compare and contrast ancient and contemporary cultural products and
perspectives using independent research employing print, non-print, electronic
materials, and cultural artifacts.
3.03

civilizations, using independent research employing print, non-print, electronic
materials, and cultural artifacts.
3.04

disciplines.
3.05
