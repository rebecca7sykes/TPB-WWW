INFORMATION
Turf Grass provides hands-on instruction and emphasizes eight units of
instruction including: fundamentals of soils and pests; environmental
issues related to turf management; landscape basics; lawn care and turf
production; golf course management; sports turf and turf irrigation; turf
equipment and maintenance; and human resources and financial man-
agement. Safety skills will be emphasized as well as leadership devel-
opment and work-based learning. Opportunities exist for students to
conduct internships or apprenticeships related to landscaping, lawn care,
and golf course management.
