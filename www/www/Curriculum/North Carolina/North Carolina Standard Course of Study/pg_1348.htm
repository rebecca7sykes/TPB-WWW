This course is designed to provide a foundation for students interested
in a career in travel, tourism, and recreation marketing. Emphasis is
placed on the hospitality/tourism industry, customer relations, travel
destinations, tourism promotion, economics, and career development.
Skills in mathematics, psychology, geography, and communications are
reinforced in this course. Work-based learning strategies appropriate for
this course include cooperative education or paid/unpaid internships.
Marketing simulations, projects, teamwork, DECA leadership activities,
meetings, conferences, and competitions provide many opportunities for
application of instructional competencies.
