The United States Departments of Education and Labor have initiated
public-private partnerships to develop voluntary skill standards for
various industries. Skills and performance levels needed by the
American workforce to be competitive have been identified.
The National Voluntary Occupational Skill Standards used as guides in
Trade & Industrial Education follow:

Standards for the Graphic Communication Industry.

welding technology programs.
