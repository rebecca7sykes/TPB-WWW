This course provides a basic introduction to collision repair work and
the technical aspects of the collision repair industry. Topics include
safety, hand and power tools and equipment, painting and refinishing,
welding, cutting and panel repair. Skills in mathematics, science, read-
ing, leadership, business and problem solving are reinforced. The work-
based strategies appropriate for this course are job shadowing, intern-
ships, cooperative education, and apprenticeship. Hands-on work
experiences and SkillsUSA leadership activities provide many opportu-
nities to enhance classroom instruction and career development.
