This course covers issues related to planning, management, finance,
sales, labor, technology, community, health, environment, and safety.
Topics include estimating, leveling instruments, forms, special framing,
interior and exterior finishing, cabinets, built-ins, and metal studs. Skills
in technical subjects, production, leadership, safety, problem solving,
reading, and mathematics are reinforced in this course. Work-based
learning strategies appropriate for this course are cooperative education
and apprenticeship. Hands-on work experiences and SkillsUSA leader-
ship activities provide many opportunities to enhance classroom instruc-
tion and career development.
