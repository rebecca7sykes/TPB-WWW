LOCAL COURSE
OPTIONS
Schools may offer one or more specialized courses not included in the
Standard Course of Study
. These courses should meet a local economic
need. Options may include:
Aerospace
Air Conditioning/Refrigeration
Commercial Art
Diesel Mechanics
Electro-Mechanical Technology
Law Enforcement
Marine Occupations
Photography
Plumbing
Textiles
Refer to Part I, Local Course Options, and Appendix B for instructions
on how to offer these courses.
