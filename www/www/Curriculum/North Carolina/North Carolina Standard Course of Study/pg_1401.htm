Career Exploration Clubs of North Carolina (CECNC) is a local and state career-
technical student organization for middle grades students enrolled in exploratory
career-technical education courses. The purposes of CECNC are to encourage,
enhance and reinforce instruction, develop competent leadership, create more
interest for exploring tentative occupation choices, develop character and citizen-
ship, and to encourage participation in the CTSOs at the high school level.
The program and activities of Career Exploration Clubs of North Carolina are
designed to be appropriate for middle grades students enrolled in any exploratory
Career and Technical Education course in Grades 6-8. These exploratory courses
include:
? Exploring Biotechnology
? Exploring Business Technologies
? Exploring Career Decisions
? Exploring Life Skills
? Exploring Technology Systems
CECNC activities and events are also appropriate for students enrolled in the
