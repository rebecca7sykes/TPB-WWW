delivery of compassionate, quality health care by providing opportunities for
knowledge, skill and leadership development of all Health Occupations Educa-
tion students, therefore, helping the students to meet the needs of the health
care industry.
High school males and females in grades 9 through 12 who are, or have been,
enrolled in a Health Occupations Education program.
