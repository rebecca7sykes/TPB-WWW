TSA is dedicated to helping students develop broad technological literacy to become
responsible, participating, healthy and successful citizens. As part of the state's
technology education program, TSA helps students acquire and apply design, problem-
solving, teaming and leadership skills. Students also learn to use simple and complex
tools found in communication, manufacturing, structural and transportation systems.
Students also are given the opportunity to develop authentic skills, which are reflective
of today's workplace, and to demonstrate and be recognized for excellence by others.
In addition to competitive conferences, students have the opportunity to attend regional
and state workshops, that provide leadership, teaming, and problem-solving develop-
ment.
