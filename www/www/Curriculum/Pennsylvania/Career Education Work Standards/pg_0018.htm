Venture capital:
Public or private funds invested in a potentially profitable business enterprise despite risk of loss.
Vocational rehabilitation centers:
Educational facilities that provide life skills and occupational training services for individuals
with special needs.
Wages:
Payments of money for labor or services according to contract and on an hourly, daily, or
piecework basis.
Web-based training:
Instruction that is available online.
Work habits:
Acquired behaviors that individuals regularly perform in completing tasks related to chores,
school or job.
Working conditions:
The environment in which an individual is employed.
