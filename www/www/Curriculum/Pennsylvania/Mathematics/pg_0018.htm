Academic Standards for Mathematics
15
O.
Determine the domain and range of a
relation, given a graph or set of
ordered pairs.
P.
Analyze a relation to determine
whether a direct or inverse variation
exists and represent it algebraically
and graphically.
Q.
Represent functional relationships in
tables, charts and graphs.
R.
Create and interpret functional models.
S.
Analyze properties and relationships of
functions (e.g., linear, polynomial,
rational, trigonometric, exponential,
logarithmic).
T.
Analyze and categorize functions by
their characteristics.
