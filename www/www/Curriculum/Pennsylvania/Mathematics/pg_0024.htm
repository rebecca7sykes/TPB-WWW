Customary system:
A system of weights and measures frequently used in the United States. The basic unit of weight is the pound; the
basic unit of capacity is the quart.
Deductive reasoning
: The process of reasoning from statements accepted as true to reach a conclusion.
Direct variation:
Two variables are so related that their ratio remains constant.
Domain:
The set of all possible values for the unknown in an open sentence.

A statement of equality between two mathematical expressions (e.g., X + 5 = Y - 2).
