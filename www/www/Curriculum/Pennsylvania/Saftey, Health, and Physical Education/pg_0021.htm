Academic Standards for Health, Safety and Physical Education
22 Pa. Code, Chapter 4, Appendix D (#006-276) Final Form-Annex A July 18, 2002 Page # 19
Vigorous physical activity: Sustained, repetitive, large muscle movements (e.g., running, swimming, soccer) done at 60% or more
of maximum heart rate for age. Maximum heart rate is 220 beats per minute minus the participant's
age. Activity makes person sweat and breathe hard.
